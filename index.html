Our Story

Six floors of Alpine
grandeur

Perched above the shimmering Lake Saint Moritz, the Crystal Alpine Palace rises six stories through alpine meadows and eternal snow. Each of our 120 meticulously appointed rooms frames a private panorama of the Engadin Valley — a living canvas that changes with every season.

From intimate suites to spacious family sanctuaries, every accommodation is crafted with the finest Swiss craftsmanship, ensuring your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.
